Built for every bidder

Tender submissions across five sectors, handled the same simple way

Whichever sector you're bidding into, the submission flow and document checklist adapt to what that tender requires.

Government

National and provincial department tenders and RFQs.

  • Registration & tax clearance
  • BBBEE / compliance certificate
  • Bid documents (SBD forms)

Municipal

City and local municipality service & supply tenders.

  • Municipal service provider registration
  • Local labour / SMME declarations
  • Rates clearance certificate

Construction

Civil, building and infrastructure project tenders.

  • CIDB grading certificate
  • Method statement & programme
  • Health & safety file

Mining

Mining, exploration and mineral supply-chain tenders.

  • Mine Health & Safety compliance
  • Environmental management plan
  • Equipment & competency records

Private Sector

Corporate RFPs, RFQs and vendor onboarding tenders.

  • Company profile & references
  • Insurance & liability cover
  • Custom scope-of-work response

Not sure which fits?

Select "Other" on the submission form and describe the tender — we'll route it correctly.
