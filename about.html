About

A simpler way to submit tender documents

Zabetha Solutions was built to remove the friction from tender submissions. Instead of chasing email threads and juggling attachments across government, municipal, construction, mining and private-sector opportunities, bidders get one guided form with a checklist tailored to the sector they're bidding into.

Every submission is captured with full company, contact and tender-reference details alongside the uploaded documents, so nothing gets lost and every party has a clear record of what was submitted and when.

What we help with

  • Structured tender document collection, sector by sector
  • A single point of submission instead of scattered emails
  • Confirmation on receipt, so you know your bid is in
